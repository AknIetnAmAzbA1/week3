Interesting Facts About Michigan

Geography

Michigan is known for its Great Lakes, with more than 3,200 miles of shoreline.

Famous Cities

  • Detroit - The Motor City
  • Ann Arbor - Home of the University of Michigan
  • Grand Rapids - Known for its art and furniture industry

Tourist Attractions

Mackinac Island is a popular tourist destination with no cars allowed.

State Symbols

The state bird of Michigan is the American Robin.
